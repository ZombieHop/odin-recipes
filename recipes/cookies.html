Cookies

Description

An easy recipe for cookies.

Ingredients

  • 275g butter
  • 275g brown sugar
  • 225g granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 475g plain flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 300g milk chocolate

Steps

  1. Beat butter and sugars with a whisk.
  2. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing them each time. Once combined add vanilla extract.
  3. Add flour and baking powder and mix with a wooden spoon. Add chocolate chunks and mix again.
  4. Chill dough for 30 minutes.
  5. Preheat oven to 180C.
  6. Slice dough into discs and place on tray.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes.
