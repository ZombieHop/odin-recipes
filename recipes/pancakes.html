Pancakes

Description

An easy American pancake recipe.

Ingredients

  • 135g plain flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp caster sugar
  • 130ml milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp butter

Steps

  1. Sift the flour, baking powder, salt and caster sugar into a bowl. In a separate bowl whisk together the milk and egg, then whisk in the butter.
  2. Add it to the flour mixture and beat until smooth.
  3. Heat a frying pan and add butter. When melted add batter.
  4. Wait for about 3 minutes before flipping over and cooking for another two minutes.
