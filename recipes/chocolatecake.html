Chocolate Cake

Description

An easy chocolate cake recipe.

Ingredients

  • 225g plain flour
  • 350g caster sugar
  • 85g cocoa powder
  • 1.5 tsp baking powder
  • 1.5 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 2 eggs
  • 250ml milk
  • 125ml vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 250ml boiling water
  • 200g chocolate
  • 200ml double cream

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C and line two tins.
  2. Put all ingredients except boiling water into a large mixing bowl and beat until smooth.
  3. Add the boiling water a little at a time until smooth.
  4. Pour into tins and bake in the oven for 25-30 minutes.
  5. Remove and cool completely before icing.
